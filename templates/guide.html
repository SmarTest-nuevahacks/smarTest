Make your tests uncheatable!

1. Don't share answers with anyone

The easiest mistake is to tell students what they can expect. It's better to surprise them and prevent them from cheating!

2. Create uncheatable questions

Create questions, which require thinking and not only knowledge - they are much harder to be cheated by students because they have to think. They also reduce the likelihood of cheating as students aren't cored by the knowledge!

3. Set a strict time limit

Cheating takes a lot more time than simply writing answers so set a time limit that is just right to complete the answers!

4. Ask your instituton admin for help

Let your institution's IT admin help you with any problems you might have and ask for tutorials to better understand the program

5. Ask your students for solutions, not answers

If you ask your students for answers, they can simply copy those from the internet. However, if you ask for solutions you have more proof that it was their own work! And if not, it's easier to check for plaguirism

6. Create the questions yourself

Don't take questions from the internet, as in that case you are giving your studemts a resource to cheat from. Create your own, custom questions!
